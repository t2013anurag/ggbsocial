Value education on caring for the aged

We all are affected by what we learn at a young age. Abhaya “Value Education on caring for the Aged” programme’s main focus is to sensitize school children and general populace on ageing issues so they treat elderly with love, care and respect and understand their issues in depth. The Abhaya team is meeting up with educationists, school principals and policy makers, urging them to include Value Education on Age care in school curriculums.
