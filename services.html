Services to Senior citizens

In the modern society, old family values are taking a beating. There is a great rush for material wealth and often the greed drives young people to frenetic life styles that exclude the roles of old people. Many times, especially in cities with the highly stressful jobs and career pursuits, the young, though willing, are unable to give sufficient attention to their parents. The rapidly changing social mores are making compatibility of young and old difficult. In addition to this, many families have working couples needing to earn to make both ends meet; thus they remain away from homes for long hours leaving senior citizens to fend for themselves. Old persons, especially very old persons find it lonely and difficult to spend long hours without someone to take care of them. The problem is compounded if they are bedridden. Unlike some Western countries which have a well laid out old age structure and schemes that assure excellent and high quality senior citizen homes with all amenities and services to take care of their needs and old age worries, India has very few good quality homes serving needy senior citizens of our country. We aim to provide the best of comfort, affection and health and other related facilities to these old people in the ambience of green and pleasant nature and also ensure a homely atmosphere. It is our belief that many old people may be physically weak but mentally capable and have much to impart to the young given the right circumstances. Their services, where suitable and willing, shall be utilized in our activities providing a meaningful purpose to their lives.
